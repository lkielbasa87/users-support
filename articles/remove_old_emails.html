How to delete old e-mails in order to regain space?

Logging into your mailbox

In order to delete old e-mails log in to your server and click on mail link in upper left corner of your browser.

Deleting mails

After logging into server mark latest e-mails and delete them.

Emptying recycle bin

After deleting those mails you have to empty your recycle bin in order to regain your space.

Pay attention - if your mailbox will be full for too long time you'll stop receiving new mails. You may lose key messages.
